Violations Types

A Violation by Category: Our county legislation recognizes various types of violations that depend on steady or moving vehicles, a vehicle's relationship with pedestrians and other vehicles, etc.

Stop Sign: When coming to a Stop Sign, a vehicle operator is required to stop completely regardless of anything else going on.

Speed: In most streets and roads of our county, speed limit signs are clearing posted. Except for emergency vehicles, every car is required to obey those speed limits.

Red Light: A red light is probably the highest signal of a dangerous situation. In fact, there are various types of infractions related to a red light. The most classic rule is that, when coming to a traffic signal that has a red light, whether steady or blinking, the vehicle must completely stop.
